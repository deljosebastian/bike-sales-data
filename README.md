# bike-sales-data
The bike sales data gives an in-depth analysis of bike sales in different countries using an Excel dataset.
THE EXCEL FILE CONTAINS THE FOLLOWING COLUMNS
  . Martial status
  . Gender
  . Income
  . children
  . Education
  . Occupation
  . Homeowners
  . Cars owned
  . Commute distance
  . Region
  . Age
   Purchased bike
